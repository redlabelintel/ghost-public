### **Detailed Pet Import Requirements**

#### **Step 1: Microchip Identification**
- **Requirement:** 15-digit ISO 11784/11785 compliant microchip
- **Timing:** Must be implanted before any documentation
- **Cost:** $50-75 at veterinary clinic
- **Documentation:** Microchip certificate linking to all health records
- **Registration:** Update microchip database with Georgia contact information

#### **Step 2: Vaccination Protocol**
**CORE REQUIREMENT - Rabies Vaccination:**
- **Timing:** Between 21 days and 11 months prior to travel
- **Documentation:** Rabies certificate signed and stamped by veterinarian
- **Current Status Check:** Verify Saffy's rabies vaccination is within window

**REQUIRED ADDITIONAL VACCINATIONS:**
- **Distemper, Hepatitis, Leptospirosis, Parvovirus, Parainfluenza**
- **Timing:** No more than 11 months before import
- **Documentation:** Must be recorded on international health certificate

#### **Step 3: Rabies Titer Test**
- **Requirement:** NOT required for dogs from any country
- **Recommendation:** Consider for return travel safety (Georgia classified as high-risk rabies country)
- **Process:** Blood test 30+ days after most recent rabies vaccination
- **Cost:** $150-200 if pursued

#### **Step 4: Parasite Treatments**
**MANDATORY INTERNAL PARASITE TREATMENT:**
- **Target:** Echinococcus multilocularis (tapeworm)
- **Timing:** Shortly before travel (veterinarian guidance)
- **Documentation:** Record on health certificate or separate treatment record

**RECOMMENDED EXTERNAL PARASITE TREATMENT:**
- **Target:** Fleas, ticks
- **Timing:** Before travel
- **Products:** Veterinarian-recommended treatments

#### **Step 5: International Health Certificate**
**CRITICAL DOCUMENT - Must be completed within 10 days of travel**
- **Issued by:** Licensed veterinarian in Spain
- **Endorsed by:** Spanish government veterinarian (SENASA)
- **Contents:** Complete pet identification, vaccination records, health attestation
- **Validity:** 10 days from endorsement to Georgia entry

#### **Step 6: No Import Permit Required**
- **Advantage:** Accompanied pets don't need advance import permits
- **Process:** Documentation review at Tbilisi Airport upon arrival
- **Backup:** Unaccompanied pets would require permits (not applicable)
