## Summary

**Framework:** A roadmap for personal development in the AI era, emphasizing human-AI collaboration over replacement.

## Key Insights

### 1. The Human 3.0 Framework
| Version | Characteristics | Era |
|---------|-----------------|-----|
| Human 1.0 | Manual, analog, isolated | Pre-internet |
| Human 2.0 | Digital, connected, automated | Internet era |
| Human 3.0 | AI-leveraged, amplified, hybrid | Current |

### 2. Core Principles
- **Leverage over labor:** Use AI for scale, humans for direction
- **Creativity over computation:** Humans provide vision, AI provides execution
- **Strategy over tactics:** Focus on high-level decisions
- **Meta-skills over hard skills:** Learn to direct AI, not just do tasks

### 3. Top 1% Implications
The "top 1%" in the AI era isn't defined by raw output but by:
- Quality of AI direction
- Unique human insight
- Strategic positioning
- Network effects
